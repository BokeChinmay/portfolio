Who Am I?

Name: Chinmay Gaikwad
Game Design and Development Student at Rochester Institute of Technology.
Interests: Video Games, Media, Psychology, Philosophy, Languages, Creative Writing
Life Goals: Sustainable Life and a Successful Career. Hope to work on my own dream game project.

Introduction to Video Games

  • I love to indulge in new experiences. I love to understand about the world, things I take an interest in, and to explore and learn.
  • My love for video games began from a young age, when I would visit my friend's house and we would play video games together for hours.
  • My parents were always conservative about allowing to play games, as they thought it would deter me from my studies. They looked at it like a distraction.
  • Ironically, I'm trying to make a career around video games now.
  • Video games were an escape from reality for me, as they let me immerse myself into this world separate from us, which was therapeutic for me.

Introduction to Other Forms of Media

  • My passion for video games, drifted into other mediums of entertainment, ranging from cartoons, anime, movies, series and to some extent books.
  • I'd love to get lost in these fictional as well as non-fictional worlds.
  • To understand the meaning behind the art that is writing, the different forms of portraying your ideas and the various ways that media expands, all of it, is fascinating.
  • Furthermore, to experience the stories and understand the characters in depth, makes me genuinely feel at peace.

Psychology and Philosophy

  • All interest in understanding characters and the layers behind stories, made me super interested in subjects like psychology and philosophy.
  • This goes to the extent that I'm considering majoring in Psychology to better enhance my knowledge on the subject.
  • I also love to see different philosophies and ideologies, understand their meanings.
  • Both of these interests, help me understand characters and stories better.

Creative Writing

  • Since my interest in media has been huge ever since I was a little kid, I used to love to write fictional stories, or spin offs based off of shows I liked.
  • My thoughts are always racing, and I'm always trying to come up with new ideas.
  • One day, I wish to write my own video game, from the characters to the story. It is a sort of a dream project of mine.
  • For this, I'm planning to minor in creative writing to improve my writing as well as to help me get closer to achieving that dream.
